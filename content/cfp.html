Call for Papers


Hong Kong, China
October 28-30, 2016
http://phdforum.se.cuhk.edu.hk/index.html

The International Doctoral Forum 2016 invites you to experience Hong Kong, Asia's world city, while enjoying the high quality forum programs. The objective of the forum is to bring together top Ph.D. students from all over the world, contributing to all facets of networking, big data and multimedia computing, including Algorithms and Software, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Computer Graphic, Computer Vision, Networks, Social Computing, Human Computing Interaction and Speech and Languages. All expenses in Hong Kong of the attendees directly related to the Forum will be covered by The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), including registration, meals, accommodation, and local transportation fees, etc.

IMPORTANT DATES:
- Paper submission due: 1 September 2016
- Notification of acceptance: 14 September 2016
- Author registration due: 28 September 2016
- Forum: 28-30 October 2016

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS:
- Invited talks
- Oral Presentation
- Social activities
- Local tours

PAPER SUBMISSION:

Student authors are invited to submit research papers on original work (including recently published work) describing current research and novel ideas in Multimedia, Web Mining, Networking and Big Data. Papers whose contributions are supported by experimental evaluations are strongly encouraged.

Copyright of the submitted work is held by the author/owner.

Papers are solicited in all areas of network and media computing, including, but not limited to:

Multimedia:
  • Audio, speech and natural language processing
  • Signal Processing and machine learning
  • Computer vision and image processing
  • Multimedia content analysis and indexing
  • Multimedia authoring and retrieval
  • Multimedia coding and trans-coding
  • Multi-modal human-computer interaction and integration
  • Multi-modal biometric authorization
  • Multimedia security and rights management
  • Human-computer interaction
Web Mining:
  • Web structural, content, and usage mining
  • Web information clustering and classification
  • Web page search, crawling, and ranking techniques
  • Link analysis
  • Web metrics and statistics
  • Social Computing
Networking:
  • Network architectures and algorithms
  • Cyber security
  • Mobile networking, multimedia networking, and social networking
  • Next generation networks
  • Datacenter networking
  • Green (energy-efficient) communication and networking
Big Data:
  • Big data curation and sharing
  • Efficient big data storage and processing
  • Big data analytics in special domains
  • Machine learning for big data
  • Structuring and visualization of big data
  • Security enhancement for big data
